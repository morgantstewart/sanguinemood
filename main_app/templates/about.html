About Sanguine

Sanguine is a mood tracking app that allows you to track your moods and feelings over time.

Mood monitoring can:

  • create space to feel your feelings
  • help you recognize patterns and triggers
  • help you communicate your feelings to others
  • help you get the mental health support you need
